The website was created for a psychotherapy practice. Its role is to calmly introduce visitors to the offer, show the most important information, and guide them to the next step without excess content or distracting elements. Visitors can quickly understand what the service is about and move forward in a simple way.

### Scope of work
* Landing page
* First screen with a clear message
* Responsive website layout
* Online payment integration (Stripe)
* Publication under the final domain
* Booking system: ProsteZapisy.pl
