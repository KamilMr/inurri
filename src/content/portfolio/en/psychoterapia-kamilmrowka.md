## Project goal

The website needed to introduce psychotherapy in a calm and focused way from the very first screen. The goal was to avoid unnecessary complexity and guide visitors from the opening message toward a simple next step.

## Scope

- service website / landing page implementation,
- first screen with a quote and a simple action button,
- responsive layout for desktop and mobile devices,
- simple content structure matched to the service,
- deployment under the final production domain.

## Approach

The page uses a large, calm background image and minimal typography. The first screen acts as an emotional introduction, while the “Start” button gives visitors one clear action instead of many competing choices.

This direction fits a service website where trust, readability, and a low-friction first step matter more than visual noise.

## Result

The result is a simple and focused website that presents the service clearly and helps visitors move toward the next step without distraction.
