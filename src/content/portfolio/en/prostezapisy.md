The application was created for people who accept bookings for specific time slots. It lets them share a simple calendar link with clients, accept online reservations, and receive email notifications. The client can choose an available time independently, while the person accepting bookings receives only the needed details, usually a name, email address, or phone number.

### Scope of work
* Product website
* Public booking calendar
* Online appointment booking
* Booking form without client account
* Email notifications
* User panel for managing available times
