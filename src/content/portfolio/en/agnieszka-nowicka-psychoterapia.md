## Project goal

The website needed to present a psychotherapy practice in a calm and structured way. Visitors should quickly understand who they can contact, what kind of support is available, and where the office is located.

The project was a service website for a psychotherapist working individually with adults, couples, marriages, and families. It also includes information about psycho-oncology consultations and therapy in Spanish.

## Scope

- service website implementation,
- first screen with clear identification and a calm visual background,
- sections for profile, offer, pricing, and contact,
- presentation of experience and therapeutic scope,
- social media links,
- contact form and contact details.

## Approach

The project uses a minimal layout, large visual background, and restrained typography. The opening screen creates a calm entry point, while the main navigation leads visitors to the essential information without unnecessary complexity.

For this type of website, readability and trust were the most important design goals. Instead of a complex interface, the page focuses on the visitor’s practical questions: who the specialist is, what support is offered, how much a consultation costs, and how to make contact.

## Result

The result is a clear service website for a psychotherapy practice, presenting the offer, experience, and contact information in one place. The page gives visitors quick access to the information they need before making first contact.
