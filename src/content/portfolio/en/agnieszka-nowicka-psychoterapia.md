The website was created for a private psychotherapy practice. It lets visitors learn about the therapist, check the scope of support, review pricing, and find the details needed to get in touch. Visitors can quickly see whether the offer matches their needs and how to contact the practice.

### Scope of work
* Personalized service website
* Presentation of the therapist and experience
* Description of support areas
* Pricing section
* Contact form
* Contact details and social links
* Blog link
* Individual booking view connected with ProsteZapisy.pl
